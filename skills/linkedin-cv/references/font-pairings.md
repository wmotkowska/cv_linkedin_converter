## How to Choose

1. **Start from the user's theme** — match the mood keywords above
2. **When in doubt**, default to "Classic Authority" — it works for any industry
3. **Never use more than 3 font files** — constraint breeds elegance
4. **Mix font families, not just weights** — a bold and regular of the same font is not a "pairing"
5. **Test display font at large sizes** (24-36pt) and body at reading sizes (9-11pt) — both must shine at their working size
